## How to Use

### 1. Navigate to Quiz Practice
Click "Quiz Practice" in the sidebar navigation.

### 2. Configure Your Quiz
Choose:
- Number of questions (5-100)
- Difficulty level
- Learning path

### 3. Start the Quiz
Click "Start Quiz" and begin answering questions.

### 4. Answer Questions
- Read the question carefully
- Select your answer
- Click "Submit Answer" to see if you're correct
- Read the explanation to understand why

### 5. Review Results
After completing all questions:
- View your score and time
- Review all answers
- See correct answers for missed questions
- Try again or return to settings
